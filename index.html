Why wooden furniture is the best kind

Wood is extremely resilient and requires very little maintenance. Wood is a long-lasting natural material that can stand constant abuse, whether it's spills in the kitchen or scratches in the dining room in your apartment. Solid wood furniture can last for generations with minimum care, making yout apartment beautiful.

Why Material is important

By learning which materials are often used for cheap furniture (think formica, particle board and the ilk), it will be easier to figure out the quality of what you are buying instantly. MDF, laminates, softwood, and melamine are also hallmarks of cheap furniture. Of course these materials are great for less used spaces and children's bedrooms, but if you spot these materials in cheap bedroom furniture, be sure to know that it won't be high quality. It would downgrade your whole apartment or office.

Why planning is important

For the best utilization of space, furniture is placed to allow free movement. This makes the space around it as important as its arrangement. Thus, it works as a functional and circulatory element in interior design. Interior designers use furniture to establish a pleasing sense of order in homes, apartments or offices.
